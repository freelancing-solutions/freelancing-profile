Handing Over Procedures

Once i have completed the initial production deployment in-case of Web Development, Web Application Development or API Development, the projected will be handed over to you or your company.

There are certain procedures which will need to be followed in order for this process to be completed successfully.

Domain and Email Setup

IN-Case of Web Development or Web Application Development involving a custom domain name i will setup your domain in order to take advantage of the following.
  • CloudFlare Caching to improve site speed
  • SSL Certificates (http:// to https://) for your main domain, sub-domains and API Endpoints
  • DDOS Protection Through CloudFlare
  • DKIM for your Emails, to improve Email Deliverability and Security
  • Free SMTP Server for your Emails + default email addresses for you and your stuff
  • Custom Email Templates for your business emails

Website or Web Application Health Checks and Notifications

I will help setup site or API health checks and notifications mainly by Email the notification messages will be sent to your email inbox, however you can also choose to have me get notified in-case there is a downtime or a malfunction that may cause an undesirable effect.

if possible and also dependent on your hosting service some notification messages of this kind may be delivered through your Slack APP in addition to your email address.

This option will be highly dependent on your maintenance contract see below.

Events which will be monitored may include the following:

  • Unreachable Server (or Server Error)
  • Traffic Spikes
  • Abnormal Traffic (DDOS Attacks)
  • Budget Exceeded (Hosting service Dependent)
  • Security Vulnerability (Checks & Notifications )
    1. (Requires Maintenance Contract & Agreement to be fixed)

Production & Staging Deployments

Depending on your hosting service i may setup a continuous deployment environment from your github repository to your Hosting Provider, this will allow you, me or any developer to deploy to a specific branch in your github repository and the changes will automatically be deployed to your staging environment or production environment.

Note that it may be possible to complete this step during project setup procedures, in which case you might need only to be trained and shown how to deploy to each of your enviroments or the details be supplied to you or your company.

This workflow highly simplifies:

  • Project Testing
  • The ability to roll-back deployments
  • A/B Testing for landing pages & etc
  • Fast Recovery times in-case of errors

Setup Procedures & Accounts Documentations

For the above setup procedures, instructions and accounts will be documented and handed over to you. if i am required to maintain your project then i will keep a copy of those instructions for safe keeping or in-case they are needed for maintenance purposes.

Included in the instructions will be a procedure on how to change your API Keys, Access Tokens and or usernames and passwords for added security.

Note: some setup procedures may require Teamviewer in-case you are unable to follow some of the instructions
