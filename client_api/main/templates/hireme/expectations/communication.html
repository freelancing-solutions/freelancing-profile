Communication Guidelines

Project Notifications & Progress Updates

Every-time there is an update on your project messages detailing the updated components of the said project will appear on our slack channel dedicated to your project and also on the project details page on this website.

  • Website Development Progress Messages will be auto generated from Github upon updating of your freelance project in github.
  • Payment Notifications Messages - will also be sent from our payment gateway to notify you of payment received, Invoices will be downloadable from our website from links sent together with the notifications.

Instructions on downloading setting up and configuring slack for your website development freelance project can be found on this website.

Messaging & File Uploads

Messages regarding your freelance job project (Web Development and etc.) can be sent through slack or on the website messaging tab on the freelance job detail page.

Note that messages sent on the website project page will also appear on the slack project channel and vice versa. Responses will be sent as soon as is possible.

File Uploads and Downloads are supported both on slack project channel and on the website project page Files related to:

  • Website Development (Website Logo, Information Files, etc)
  • Company Documents (Letterheads, Contact Info, Services, Offices and etc)
  • Contact Information (Related Persons Contact Information)
  • Product/Services Information (Details Regarding the Products or Services and how they are offered.)
  • Proof of Payments (Payment Information Related to Projects Only)
Can be uploaded using either slack or on the website.
