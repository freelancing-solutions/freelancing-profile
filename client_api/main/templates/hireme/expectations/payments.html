Payment Methods & Procedures

EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer)

Electronic Funds Transfer is supported in this website, once payment is made the payment receipt can be sent via the website project detail page (Messages Section) or on the Slack Project Channel (created upon freelance job submission).

EFT Payment verification
Once payment is verified it will then be credited to your freelance project, Verification may be delayed dependent on the bank and country of origin. but may not exceed seven days upon payment. delayed payments may not delay the progress of your freelance job.

Credit Card Payments

Credit Card Payments are supported only through PayPal, henceforth credit card details will not be held in this website or on any of my back end servers.

Credit Card Payment Verification
i rely upon paypal for payment verifications and will only credit your project upon receipt of successful payment.

PayPal Payments

PayPal Payments are supported and payments will be credited once payment is verified by paypal. All paypal terms and conditions for payments on services and products will be respected, this includes payment refunds.

Crypto Currency Payments

The following crypto currencies are excepted :
  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Dogecoin (Doge)
  • Monero (XMR)
  • Litecoin (LTC)

Payments with crypto currencies for freelance jobs will be calculated using the dollar exchange value of the crypto currency at the moment of payment.

If payment method is selected as crypto currency when submitting your freelance job payment amount will be calculated using the exchange value to the dollar at the time of submitting the freelance job and the amount wont change for the duration of the freelance job.

Terms and Refund Policy

Our Refund policy is such that if there is any dissatisfaction when your services are being rendered and an agreement is reached to terminate the service agreement, then refund will normally be guaranteed to half of the amount already paid. if the project has already progressed to 50% ( or half the job is already done).

Any payment agreements made with third parties will not be affected by this terms, and the terms of the third parties will apply.

Third parties include hosting services providers, Cloud Services (Google Cloud Platform and etc)
