What is Freelancing

Freelancing is a process by which you temporarily hire someone to work on your task or job, commonly known as freelance jobs, and then pay such a person or freelancer an agreed upon amount, depending on the task or freelance job and the time it takes for the person to complete such a job. after the job is completed it usually means the end of such a freelance contract.

Finding freelancers or the best freelancers for a job is the reason why there are several online freelancing sites servicing specific sectors today. it is also one of the reasons why i have created this website the other being as a means to share my knowledge and skills with the community.

Hiring Me - Steps to follow

If you have a job on Web Development which you think i can complete then you only have to

  • Create your freelancing account in this website
  • Submit your freelance job details
    1. Details on and about your Website
    2. Details about you services or products
    3. Company Contact Details and or Personal Contact Details
    4. Information on what you want to achieve with your website or web application such as Customer Support,E-Commerce, Web Presence , and etc
  • Download Slack and Accept the invitation which would have been sent upon completion of the above steps
  • Create a Github Account (If you dont already have one), from which you can monitor the progress of your site and get access to your website code
  • Register for a developer account on GCP (Google Cloud Platform) - I Prefer hosting web applications on Google Cloud Platform
  • Alternatively and depending on the projects specifications Heroku can be substituted for Google Cloud Platform
  • Downloading and setting up of Teamviewer for remote assistance & Optional Setup Procedures
  • Once the setup process is complete then i will proceed creating your project to your specific needs
